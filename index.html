How we met

Róisín and Shane first set eyes on each other at Santry stadium athletics track, back in 2006. Fortunately Róisín was not fast enough to get away and they began dating. Although Shane was locked up in boarding school for the first year of their relationship, that did not stop him from escaping every weekend to meet up with Róisín, in Dublin. They both attended university together in Dublin City University where they studied and continued to compete for the university athletics team. After a brief stint of living in Liverpool together in 2013, they returned home, saved and bought their first home in Ashbourne, Co. Meath.

In 2019, on a trip to Portugal, Shane finally popped the question to Róisín. Not wanting to wait another decade for Shane, Róisín quickly booked a wedding date!

Covid quickly dashed the hopes of a quick wedding and a new date was set. Thankfully love is patient :)

They now look forward to celebrating their relationship with friends and family as they embark on the rest of their lives together.
